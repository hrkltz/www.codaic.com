Codaic

the Low-Code IDE for iOS

Build, test, and prototype with real data — anywhere, anytime.

Features

A complete low-code environment in your pocket

  • Visual Node System
    Intuitive node-based programming, designed for your fingers — just tap and drag.
  • Lua 5.4.7 Scripting
    Powerful scripting with Lua for custom logic and advanced control.
  • Batteries Included
    Harness device features like accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer, and more.
  • Live Data View
    Real-time data presentation for instant feedback and analysis.
  • Privacy First
    Your data stays on your device — no internet or third-party service required.

Your Benefits

No Hardware Investment

Skip buying expensive sensor modules and development kits. Your iPhone has everything you need to get started.

Instant Feedback

See results in real-time as you adjust your algorithms and node configurations. No flash, compile, or upload cycles.

Portable Development

Code anywhere, anytime. Your development environment travels with you—no laptop required.

Real-World Data

Work with actual sensor data from your environment, not simulated values. Test your code under real conditions.
